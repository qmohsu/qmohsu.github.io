## Invited talk topics (typical)
- Robust GNSS positioning under multipath/NLOS
- Integrity monitoring for satellite navigation and autonomous systems
- Factor graph optimization for GNSS/INS integration (and comparison vs EKF)
- 3D mapping‑aided GNSS (3DMA GNSS) and environment-aware positioning
- Multi-sensor fusion for urban navigation (GNSS/LiDAR/IMU/camera)
- Smartphone / Android GNSS positioning and reliability
