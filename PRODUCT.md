# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Researchers, prospective students and postdoctoral fellows, academic and industry collaborators, funders, journalists, and members of the public who need to understand IPNL's people, research, outputs, resources, and current activity.

## Product Purpose

Present the Intelligent Positioning and Navigation Laboratory as a credible, current academic authority hub. The site should explain the lab's research quickly, make publications and reusable outputs easy to find, support recruitment and collaboration, and remain safe to maintain through structured Hugo content.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative, evidence-led, and welcoming. The visual language is academic and technical without feeling institutional or cold.

## Anti-references

Do not turn the site into a generic startup landing page, a decorative portfolio, or a dense publication archive that only specialists can navigate. Preserve the established IPNL identity, multilingual content, and real research imagery.

## Design Principles

- Put research evidence, people, and public outputs before decoration.
- Make every primary journey usable on small touch screens without removing content.
- Preserve multilingual parity across English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese.
- Prefer structured, reusable presentation rules over page-specific fixes.
- Keep changes small enough to review and safe enough to roll back.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain readable contrast, keyboard access, visible focus, reduced-motion support, meaningful image alternatives, touch-friendly controls, and robust layouts from 320px upward.
