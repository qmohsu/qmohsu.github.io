IPIN-Examples provides reproducible Python and Jupyter notebook implementations of the indoor-positioning and navigation algorithms presented in the [2025 Artech House book](/publications/book-2025-principles-of-indoor-positioning-and-indoor-navigation/) by Hsu, Zhang, and Wen. The repository pairs each book chapter's methods with executable code and example datasets, intended as a teaching and reproducibility resource for students and researchers entering the field. Released under the PolyForm Noncommercial license, with datasets under CC BY-NC 4.0.
