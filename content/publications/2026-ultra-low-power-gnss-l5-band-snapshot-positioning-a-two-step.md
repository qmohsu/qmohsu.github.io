**Key idea.** The two-step approach leverages the L1 band signal to perform coarse snapshot positioning, thereby refining the position estimate and time uncertainty to narrow the L5 band signal's acquisition search range. This reduces power consumption while simultaneously exploiting the L5 band's enhanced sensitivity and higher positioning accuracy. *(IEEE Internet of Things Journal, 2026, Q1.)*
