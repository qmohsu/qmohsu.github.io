**Key idea.** Multipath and NLOS are **different errors** (figure): multipath is a reflection interfering with a direct signal that still arrives; NLOS means *only* the reflection arrives. The paper analyzes and models the NLOS pseudorange error observed in a highly urbanized area.

**Why it matters.** NLOS is the dominant and hardest-to-fix GNSS error in dense cities. Understanding and modeling it — rather than lumping it in with multipath — is the prerequisite for correcting it. *(GPS Solutions, 2018, Q1.)*
