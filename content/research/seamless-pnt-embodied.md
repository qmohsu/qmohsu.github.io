We build navigation systems for real platforms: vehicles, robots, drones, smartphones, wearables, and indoor/outdoor mobile systems. These platforms require tight GNSS/INS/LiDAR/camera/UWB/Wi-Fi fusion, robustness to low-cost sensors, and seamless operation across open-sky, urban canyon, indoor, and GNSS-denied conditions.

The thread spans **smartphone-grade urban positioning** (3DMA GNSS for L5/E5a/B2a smartphone receivers; ITF-ITSP HK$801k *AI-aided Smartphone Urban Positioning for HK Smart Cities*), **autonomous-vehicle perception integration** (LiDAR + camera + GNSS + INS), **UAV navigation** (Limin Endowed Young Scholar in Aerospace Navigation), **connected-AV safety** (RGC CRF Co-PI HK$7M *Enhancing the Safety of AVs via Cross-Layer Security Hardening*), and **indoor positioning** anchored by the 2025 Artech House book *[Principles of Indoor Positioning and Indoor Navigation](/publications/book-2025-principles-of-indoor-positioning-and-indoor-navigation/)* (Hsu, Zhang, Wen) and IPIN 2024 General Chair role.
