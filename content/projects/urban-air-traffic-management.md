This program develops **robust GNSS navigation with multi-sensor fusion for low-altitude UAVs** operating under Urban Air Traffic Management (UTM) and Urban Air Mobility (UAM) frameworks. Drones operating below 300 m in dense cities face the same multipath and NLOS regime as ground vehicles, but with much tighter integrity requirements because UAV navigation failures are immediately safety-critical and visible to the airspace authority.

**Why it matters.** UTM/UAM is a 2026–2030 priority for both HK and Mainland aviation authorities. Reliable urban GNSS for drones is the missing piece between line-of-sight RC drones and scalable commercial low-altitude operations. The program extends IPNL's urban-canyon expertise from ground vehicles to the low-altitude airspace.
