This project investigates integrity monitoring within factor graph optimization frameworks for GNSS positioning. The goal is to provide reliable position solutions with quantified confidence bounds for safety-critical applications such as autonomous driving and aviation.

Research focuses on:
- Integrating integrity constraints into factor graph formulations
- Non-Gaussian error modeling for urban GNSS
- Protection level computation within optimization-based frameworks
