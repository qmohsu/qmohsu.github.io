Prof. Li-Ta Hsu travelled to Boston, USA on 20 August 2025 to serve as **external PhD examiner** for Shuo Tang's doctoral defense at [Northeastern University](https://www.northeastern.edu/). The trip continued the lab's growing international PhD-examiner service roster — Prof. Hsu has now served as external examiner at NCKU (Dr Ya-Tzu Chen, 2019), the Australian / IPIN community (James Brotchie, 2023), NTU Singapore (Weng Xu, 2024-25), ETH Zürich (Yuanxin Pan, 2024-25), and now Northeastern.

Serving as an external PhD examiner is among the most respected forms of academic peer recognition — universities select examiners whose research expertise and stature align with the candidate's thesis area, and the role carries real responsibility for assessing the doctoral work's quality and contribution.
