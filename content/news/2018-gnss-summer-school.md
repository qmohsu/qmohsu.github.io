In July 2018, Prof. Li-Ta Hsu returned as an **invited lecturer** at the **GNSS Summer School 2018**, again contributing as a guest instructor in the programme's curriculum on satellite-based positioning. As in 2017, Prof. Hsu's role was teaching at the event — IPNL was a participating speaker, not the organizing host.
