Prof. Li-Ta Hsu was invited to speak at the **Hong Kong Motor Transport and Driving Industry Workers General Union** (香港汽車交通運輸業總工會) forum titled *「智能駕駛浪潮下　汽車交通運輸業何去何從」* (*The Intelligent Driving Wave: Where is the Motor Transport Industry Heading*) on 12 July 2025.

The talk — *The Intelligent Driving Wave: Transforming Transportation Infrastructure* — bridged the lab's academic research on intelligent vehicle positioning (including the Vehicular Self-diagnosis System for Intelligent Transport line of work) with the practical concerns of the Hong Kong transport workforce. Engagements like this extend the lab's public-community service line alongside the recurring 香港青少年航天創新大賽 (HK Youth Aerospace Innovation Competition) judging role.
