IPNL participated in an innovation and entrepreneurship competition held at Songshan Lake in Dongguan, one of the premier high-tech development zones in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area. The Songshan Lake High-tech Industrial Development Zone has grown into a major hub for advanced technology companies, research laboratories, and innovation-driven enterprises, making it a fitting venue for showcasing cutting-edge positioning and navigation technologies.

The IPNL team presented their research on intelligent positioning solutions for urban environments, demonstrating the practical applications of GNSS and sensor fusion technologies. Competitions of this kind play an important role in bridging academic research with industry needs, allowing university teams to gain feedback from industry experts and explore paths toward technology transfer and commercialization.

![Competition](/images/news/20180112b.jpg)

The event brought together teams from universities and research institutions across the Greater Bay Area, highlighting the region's commitment to fostering innovation in science and technology. For IPNL, the experience reinforced the real-world relevance of their research and opened doors for potential collaborations with industry partners in the Dongguan technology ecosystem.
