Prof. Li-Ta Hsu gave an invited workshop talk at the **[IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC) 2022](https://ieee-itss.org/conf/itsc/)** in Macau on 8 October 2022 (virtual delivery). The 30-minute talk — *Urban GNSS RTK: Challenges and Opportunities* — addressed the difficulties of achieving centimeter-level GNSS positioning in dense urban environments where multipath and NLOS effects dominate.

IEEE ITSC is the flagship annual conference of the [IEEE Intelligent Transportation Systems Society](https://ieee-itss.org/), and a recurring venue for the lab's vehicular positioning research — engagements include ITSC 2017 Yokohama (Session Chair + conference paper), ITSC 2022 Macau, ITSC 2023 Workshop on GNSS Urban Positioning, ITSC 2024 Edmonton, and ITSC 2025.
