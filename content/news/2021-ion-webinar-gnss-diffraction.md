Prof. Li-Ta Hsu and PhD student Hoi-Fung Ng delivered a 60-minute **[ION Webinar](https://www.ion.org/publications/webinars.cfm)** on 14 September 2021 to the global [Institute of Navigation](https://www.ion.org/) community. The topic — *Performance Assessment of GNSS Diffraction Models in Urban Areas* — extended the lab's 3DMA GNSS research line to characterize how well existing diffraction-loss models match measured GNSS behavior in real urban canyons. The underlying research was published in *[NAVIGATION: Journal of the Institute of Navigation](https://navi.ion.org/content/68/2/369)* (Vol. 68 No. 2, 2021) by Zhang and Hsu; the webinar recording is archived on the [ION YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@instofnavigation) alongside the rest of the ION webinar series.

This was the second of the lab's ION Webinar series engagements in 2021 — following the [June 2021 EKF-vs-FGO Webinar](/news/2021-ion-webinar-ekf-vs-fgo/) — and continued the lab's expanding visibility within the global ION community.
