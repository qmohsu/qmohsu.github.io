Prof. Li-Ta Hsu and members of PolyU IPNL took part in the **2026 [ION Pacific PNT Conference](https://www.ion.org/pnt/)** in Honolulu, Hawaii in April 2026. ION Pacific PNT is the biennial Pacific-region edition of the [Institute of Navigation](https://www.ion.org/)'s flagship technical conference series, complementing the annual ION ITM (San Diego) and ION GNSS+ conferences.

At this edition Prof. Hsu served as a **session chair** and **speaker**, and IPNL members presented their research to the international positioning, navigation and timing community. The trip continued the lab's sustained engagement with the ION Pacific PNT series — Prof. Hsu has been a [recurring contributor at prior editions](/news/2019-ion-pacific-pnt-honolulu-keynote/), including the Honolulu 2019 meeting.
