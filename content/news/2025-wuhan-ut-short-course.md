Prof. Li-Ta Hsu delivered a short course on **Factor Graph Optimization (FGO) for Seamless Positioning** at [Wuhan University of Technology (WUT)](https://english.whut.edu.cn/) on 11 July 2025, as part of WUT's 2025 Summer Excellence Academy (暑期卓越学堂) international guest-lecture programme.

The course covered the lab's research on factor-graph optimization applied to GNSS/INS/visual seamless positioning across urban-canyon, indoor, and transition environments. This was Hsu's third Wuhan engagement of recent years — following the prior Wuhan University talk in 2026 and CAS Innovation Academy of Precision Measurement Science and Technology PNT forum in April 2026.
