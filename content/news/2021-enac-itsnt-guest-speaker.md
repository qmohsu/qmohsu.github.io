Prof. Li-Ta Hsu served as Guest Speaker at the [2021 International Technical Symposium on Navigation and Timing (ITSNT 2021)](https://www.itsnt.fr/), hosted by [ENAC (École Nationale de l'Aviation Civile)](https://www.enac.fr/en/) in Toulouse, France, on 24 June 2021 (virtual delivery). The talk — *AI in GNSS and Collaborative 3D Mapping* — covered the lab's emerging research on machine-learning-aided GNSS positioning and the use of collaborative 3D maps to mitigate urban-canyon NLOS errors.

This was the lab's **first major European invited-speaking engagement** — a flagship first-of-kind that set up a series of subsequent European visits in following years (i.c.sens Hannover virtual talk later in 2021; first physical Hannover visit December 2022; Munich SatNav Summit panel-chair role in March 2025).

ENAC is France's national civil-aviation university and the ITSNT symposium is a long-running technical event in the European navigation community.
