Prof. Li-Ta Hsu is co-organizing the [1st Workshop on Robot Meets GNSS and Ranging for Seamless Autonomy: Exploring Synergies and Challenges of Radio-based Ranging Technologies in Robotics](https://robotmeetsranging.tech/), a full-day workshop at the [IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2026](https://2026.ieee-icra.org/) in Vienna, Austria, on **5 June 2026**.

The workshop bridges two communities — satellite navigation / radio-ranging and robotics — to explore how positioning technologies like GNSS, UWB, and 5G NR can be tightly coupled with robot perception, planning, and control. The proposal was selected from a record 152 submissions to ICRA 2026, of which only 67 (44%) were accepted. The international co-organizer team spans the [IEEE Robotics and Automation Society](https://www.ieee-ras.org/) community, with affiliations across The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Technical University of Munich, University of Bonn, Nanyang Technological University, German Aerospace Center (DLR), Chiba Institute of Technology, and Simon Fraser University.

Workshop website: <https://robotmeetsranging.tech/>.
