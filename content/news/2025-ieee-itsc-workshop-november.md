Prof. Li-Ta Hsu gave an invited workshop talk at the **[IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC) 2025](https://ieee-itss.org/conf/itsc/)** on 18 November 2025. The talk — *When IV (Intelligent Vehicles) Meets Urban: Integrity-Estimation Research* — built on the lab's integrity-constrained factor graph optimization research line, including work that has flowed through PLANS 2023/2024/2025 and the 2024 UCalgary invited seminar.

IEEE ITSC is the flagship annual conference of the [IEEE Intelligent Transportation Systems Society](https://ieee-itss.org/) and a recurring venue for the lab's vehicular positioning research — previous ITSC engagements include the 2022 Macau workshop, 2023 ITSC Workshop on GNSS Urban Positioning, and 2024 ITSC Edmonton talk on *Embracing the Power of Non-Gaussian Modeling*.
