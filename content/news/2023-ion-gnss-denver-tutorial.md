Prof. Li-Ta Hsu had a multi-role contribution at **[ION GNSS+ 2023](https://www.ion.org/gnss/)** in Denver, Colorado, in September 2023:

**Pre-Conference Tutorial Lecturer (3 hours)** on *Indoor Navigation and Positioning* — the third year in Prof. Hsu's recurring tutorial role at ION GNSS+ (3.5-hour editions in 2020 and 2021 (virtual); 3-hour edition at Denver 2023; later 1.5-hour edition at Baltimore 2024).

**Session Chair role** and **Track Chair role** at the conference — continuing recurring service contributions across ION GNSS+ editions.

ION GNSS+ is widely regarded as the premier annual gathering for the global GNSS technical community, with hundreds of paper presentations across parallel tracks each year.
