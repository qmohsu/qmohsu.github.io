Prof. Li-Ta Hsu contributed to **[ION GNSS+ 2023](https://www.ion.org/gnss/)** in Denver, Colorado in September 2023 in two service roles:

**Session Chair** and **Track Chair** at the conference — continuing recurring contributions across ION GNSS+ editions.

ION GNSS+ is widely regarded as the premier annual gathering for the global GNSS technical community, with hundreds of paper presentations across parallel tracks each year.
