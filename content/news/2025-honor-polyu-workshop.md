PolyU IPNL hosted a **joint research workshop with [Honor](https://www.hihonor.com/global/)** — one of China's major smartphone manufacturers — at PolyU on 29 September 2025. The workshop presented the lab's GNSS, navigation, factor-graph optimization, and AI-aided positioning research portfolio to Honor's research and engineering teams.

This Honor engagement extends the lab's broader industry partnership pattern with major handset / device manufacturers — including the long-running Huawei collaboration (since 2018, including the multi-year framework agreements 2020-2023 and 2023-2026), Google Research partnerships (since 2023 visiting researcher; 2024 Research Award), Airoha Taiwan, MediaTek, and OPPO discussions.
