PolyU IPNL hosted a **joint research workshop with [Honor](https://www.hihonor.com/global/)** — one of China's major smartphone manufacturers — at PolyU on 29 September 2025. The workshop presented the lab's GNSS, navigation, factor-graph optimization, and AI-aided positioning research portfolio to Honor's research and engineering teams, and discussed potential research-collaboration directions.
