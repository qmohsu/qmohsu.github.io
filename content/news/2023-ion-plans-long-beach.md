PolyU IPNL had multiple contributions at the **2023 [IEEE/ION Position, Location and Navigation Symposium (PLANS)](https://www.ion.org/plans/)** held in Long Beach, California in April 2023:

**Best Track Paper Award.** Yuan Luo's paper *Super-Resolution GPS Receiver: User's Acceleration Computation* received the [PLANS 2023 Best Track Paper Award](https://www.ion.org/) — selected from over 200 paper submissions. Prof. Hsu served as supervisor / co-author on the award-winning work.

**NAVIGATION Editorial Board Meeting.** Prof. Hsu attended the [NAVIGATION: Journal of the Institute of Navigation](https://www.ion.org/navi/) Editorial Board meeting (24 April 2023) in his capacity as Associate Editor — a role he was promoted to from prior Editorial Board membership.

**Multiple lab papers presented.** Beyond the award-winning paper, several other lab papers were on the PLANS 2023 program (Zhang's LiDAR FGO outliers work; Xia's Integrity-constrained FGO; Yan's Vehicle Dynamic Model integration).
