Dr. Li-Ta Hsu and Dr. Weisong Wen received a **TechConnect 2021 Global Innovation Award** in the area of **AI, Data, Cyber & Software** at the Washington DC TechConnect World Innovation Conference and Expo.

The recognized technology, *3D LiDAR Aided GNSS Precise Positioning for L4 Autonomous Driving*, connects LiDAR perception with GNSS precise positioning. The PolyU AAE announcement describes the system as supporting centimetre-level positioning for mobile mapping systems used to build high-definition maps for Level-4 autonomous-driving vehicles in urban environments.

The recognition highlights IPNL's work on environment-aware GNSS: using scanning LiDAR point clouds to perceive the surrounding environment and improve GNSS positioning in dense cities.
