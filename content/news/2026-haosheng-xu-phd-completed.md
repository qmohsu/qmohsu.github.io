Congratulations to **Dr. Haosheng Xu**, who has completed his PhD in the Intelligent Positioning and Navigation Lab (IPNL) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

His thesis, *"Sky Visibility Estimation Using Satellite Signals for Building Update Detection,"* estimates sky visibility from GNSS satellite signals to detect changes in the urban building environment.
