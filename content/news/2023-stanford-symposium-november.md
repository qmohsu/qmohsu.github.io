Prof. Li-Ta Hsu gave a dinner talk at the **[17th Annual Stanford PNT Symposium](https://scpnt.stanford.edu/about-annual-pnt-symposium/2023-pnt-symposium)** at the Kavli Auditorium, SLAC National Laboratory, on 1-2 November 2023 — his second [Stanford Center for Position, Navigation, and Time (SCPNT)](https://scpnt.stanford.edu/) engagement of the year, following the [April 2023 Stanford GPS Lab invited seminar](/news/2023-stanford-gps-lab-first-visit/). The dinner banquet was held at the Stanford Faculty Club on the Wednesday evening of the symposium.

The talk topic — *Vision-Sensor-Aided GNSS RTK* — covered the lab's research on tight integration of visual feature sensing with GNSS real-time-kinematic positioning, with applications to autonomous driving in challenging urban environments where pure GNSS is unreliable.
