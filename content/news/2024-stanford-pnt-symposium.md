Prof. Li-Ta Hsu attended the **2024 Stanford PNT Symposium** at Stanford University on 25 October 2024 — an invited engagement extending the lab's ongoing relationship with the [Stanford Center for Position, Navigation, and Time (SCPNT)](https://scpnt.stanford.edu/) community.

The Stanford PNT Symposium is an annual day-long event bringing together the world's leading GNSS, navigation, and timing researchers. The lab's Stanford engagement began with Prof. Hsu's first invited talk at the [Stanford GPS Lab](https://gps.stanford.edu/) in April 2023, was followed by the November 2023 Stanford PNT Symposium, and continued with this 2024 edition and the April 2025 Stanford visit en route to ION PLANS Salt Lake City.
