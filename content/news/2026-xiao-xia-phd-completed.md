Congratulations to **Dr. Xiao Xia**, who has completed his PhD in the Intelligent Positioning and Navigation Lab (IPNL) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

His thesis, *"Integrity-constrained Robust Estimation for GNSS Positioning in Urban Scenarios,"* develops robust, integrity-aware estimation methods for reliable GNSS positioning in challenging urban environments.
