Prof. Li-Ta Hsu gave an invited talk at [Airoha Technology Corporation](https://www.airoha.com/) in Taiwan on 15 July 2025. The talk — *Indoor Positioning and Long-Coherent Integration for GNSS* — combined the lab's super-resolution GPS receiver research (Yuan Luo line of work, originating the 2023 PLANS Best Track Paper Award) with indoor-positioning technology directions.

This was the lab's third invited engagement with Airoha — following earlier invited talks in 2022 (virtual, 60 min) and 2021 (60 min) — reflecting an ongoing industry partnership with one of Taiwan's major fabless semiconductor companies in the wireless and audio space.
