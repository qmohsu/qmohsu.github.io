Prof. Li-Ta Hsu gave his **first invited talk** to the [i.c.sens DFG Research Training Group](https://www.icsens.uni-hannover.de/) at [Leibniz University Hannover](https://www.uni-hannover.de/en/), Germany, on 21 May 2021 (virtual delivery, 60 min). The talk — *3D LiDAR Aided GNSS via Factor Graph Optimization* — covered the lab's emerging research on tightly integrating LiDAR perception with GNSS pseudorange measurements using FGO.

This virtual engagement set up an enduring multi-year collaboration: a [first physical Hannover visit in December 2022](/news/2022-hannover-first-physical-visit/), and a [90-minute invited talk during the European tour in March 2025](/news/2025-european-tour-march/) — three engagements with the same German research community spanning four years.
