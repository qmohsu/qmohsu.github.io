PolyU IPNL had a two-day presence in Singapore at the end of July 2025.

**29 July — Invited seminar at NTU.** Prof. Li-Ta Hsu delivered an invited seminar at [Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Singapore](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/) — *Super-Resolution GPS Receivers: A New Potential for GNSS-Based Earth Observation* — drawing on the lab's long-coherent GNSS receiver design work, building on the Yuan Luo-led 2023 PLANS Best Track Paper Award on super-resolution GPS receiver acceleration computation.

**30 July — ICASSE 2025.** The following day, Prof. Hsu was an invited speaker at the editors' dialogue session of the **International Conference on Aerospace System Science and Engineering ([ICASSE 2025](https://icasse2025.com/))**, drawing on his Associate Editor roles at *NAVIGATION: Journal of the Institute of Navigation* and *IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems*. PhD student Penghui Xu presented *AI-Driven Innovations in Satellite Navigation: Overcoming Challenges for Precision and Security* — extending the lab's machine-learning-aided GNSS positioning work to robustness and security concerns.
