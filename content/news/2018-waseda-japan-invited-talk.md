Prof. Li-Ta Hsu was invited to give a seminar at [Waseda University](https://www.waseda.jp/top/en/) in Tokyo, Japan, on 14 September 2018. The visit continued the lab's engagement with the Japanese navigation research community — building on Prof. Hsu's earlier JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship at The University of Tokyo's Institute of Industrial Science (IIS), and his prior visiting scholar role at Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology.

Japan-Hong Kong navigation research engagement has remained a recurring theme — including subsequent UTokyo IIS workshop (2019) and the August 2025 travel to Osaka and Hiroshima.
