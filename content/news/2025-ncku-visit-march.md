In March 2025, Prof. Li-Ta Hsu visited his alma mater, [National Cheng Kung University (NCKU)](https://www.ncku.edu.tw/en/), in Tainan, Taiwan. NCKU's Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics is where Hsu earned both his BSc and PhD degrees (2007 and 2013 respectively), and the lab maintains long-standing collaborative ties with the navigation and aerospace research groups there.

This was a recurring connection — earlier NCKU visits in this series include 2018 (Strategic Partnership Forum with PolyU FENG dean), 2019, and prior years.
