Dr. Li-Ta Hsu received PolyU's **President's Award for Outstanding Achievement in Knowledge Transfer** for *3D Mapping Aided GNSS for Effective Positioning and Navigation in Urban Areas*. The official [PolyU KTEO](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/kteo/) award page lists the work under the **Industry I Award: Individual** category and identifies Dr. Hsu as the awardee.

The award recognizes the lab's 3D mapping-aided GNSS technology for positioning in dense urban areas such as Hong Kong and Tokyo. The work connects long-running urban GNSS research with practical knowledge transfer: using 3D city information to improve satellite positioning where buildings cause severe signal blockage, reflection, and non-line-of-sight reception.

PolyU later featured the recognition in its President's Awards knowledge-transfer showcase, and the associated PolyU video presents the work as an urban-positioning impact story for navigation in city environments.
