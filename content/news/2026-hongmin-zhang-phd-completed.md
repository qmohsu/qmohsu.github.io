Congratulations to **Dr. Hongmin Zhang**, who has completed a PhD in the Intelligent Positioning and Navigation Lab (IPNL) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

The thesis, *"Advanced Stochastic Modeling for Urban GNSS PPP-RTK Aided by 3D City Model,"* develops advanced stochastic models that strengthen GNSS PPP-RTK positioning in challenging urban environments by drawing on 3D city models.
