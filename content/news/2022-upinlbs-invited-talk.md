Prof. Li-Ta Hsu was an invited speaker at **UPINLBS 2022** (Ubiquitous Positioning, Indoor Navigation and Location-Based Services) on 19 March 2022 (virtual delivery). The 30-minute talk — *Factor Graph Optimization and its use in GNSS Collaborative Positioning and Sensor Integration* — covered the lab's foundational research on factor-graph-optimization-based GNSS positioning, which had been published in 2021 in the IEEE PLANS proceedings.

UPINLBS is a conference series focused on ubiquitous positioning and indoor navigation, drawing the Chinese and Asia-Pacific positioning research community alongside the global navigation community.
