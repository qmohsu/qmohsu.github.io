Prof. Li-Ta Hsu made his **first physical visit** to the [i.c.sens DFG Research Training Group](https://www.icsens.uni-hannover.de/) at [Leibniz University Hannover](https://www.uni-hannover.de/en/), Germany, on 6 December 2022. The visit included an invited seminar talk introducing the lab's research, and laid the groundwork for an ongoing multi-year German collaboration.

The Hannover engagement had started earlier with a virtual invited talk in May 2021 (*3D LiDAR Aided GNSS via FGO*) under an [RGC](https://www.ugc.edu.hk/eng/rgc/) / DAAD joint funding mechanism. The first physical visit in December 2022 was made possible after the lifting of pandemic-era travel restrictions, and was followed by return engagements: March 2025 (90-min invited talk at i.c.sens on *Bridging the Gap: High-Integrity GNSS RTK for Urban Robotics*) and continued exchanges into 2026.
