Prof. Li-Ta Hsu attended the **2023 [Institute of Navigation International Technical Meeting (ION ITM)](https://www.ion.org/itm/)** in Long Beach, California in January 2023. ION ITM is the Institute of Navigation's annual technical meeting focused on a broad range of navigation, positioning, and timing research.

The 2023 ITM week included preliminary discussions around the [2023 ION Per Enge Early Achievement Award](/news/2023-per-enge-award/), which would be formally presented to Prof. Hsu later in the year. Prof. Hsu was also a Session Chair at ITM (continuing his multi-year session-chair role across ION ITM 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023).
