Prof. Li-Ta Hsu delivered a **60-minute [ION Webinar](https://www.ion.org/webinar.cfm)** on 30 March 2022 to the global Institute of Navigation community. The topic — *Urban positioning: 3D-Map-Aided GNSS using dual-frequency pseudorange* — covered the lab's 3DMA GNSS research line, which combines 3D city models with multi-frequency GNSS measurements to mitigate non-line-of-sight (NLOS) and multipath errors in dense urban canyons. The recording is available on the **[ION YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WQSYLD6wJos)**.

The ION Webinar series reaches a global audience of GNSS researchers and practitioners. The webinar followed Prof. Hsu's appointment to the Associate Editor role at *[NAVIGATION: Journal of the Institute of Navigation](https://www.ion.org/navi/)* and continued the lab's expanding engagement with [Institute of Navigation](https://www.ion.org/) technical community.
