The IPNL team came together in late 2021 for a Halloween-themed celebration that brought a burst of colour and creativity to the lab. Members embraced the spirit of the occasion with costumes and decorations, transforming the usual research workspace into a festive gathering spot. The party provided a much-needed opportunity for the team to relax and enjoy each other's company, particularly after the challenges of navigating research activities during the ongoing pandemic.

Beyond the costumes and treats, the Halloween party reflected an important aspect of IPNL's culture: maintaining strong interpersonal connections within the research group. Social events like these play a valuable role in building the kind of trust and camaraderie that supports productive collaboration in the lab. For students and researchers who may spend long hours on demanding technical work, an afternoon of lighthearted fun together can go a long way toward sustaining morale and a positive team environment.
