Prof. Li-Ta Hsu visited Prof. Jianghui Geng's research group at [Wuhan University](https://www.whu.edu.cn/en/) on 22 November 2024 for a joint workshop. Prof. Geng (耿江辉) is among China's leading GNSS / geodesy researchers, and the two groups have a multi-year collaboration line including the 2022 *3D-mapping-aided PPP-RTK aiming at deep urban canyons* paper (Xin, Geng, Zhang, Ng, Guo, Hsu — *Journal of Geodesy* 96(10):78, ⭐ top-tier publication) and the 2024 *Factor Graph Optimization-based GNSS PPP-RTK* paper (Xin, Geng, Hsu — IEEE TAES).

The Wuhan engagement reflects the lab's continuing China-mainland research network alongside collaborations at SJTU, Sun Yat-Sen University, and the CAS Innovation Academy of Precision Measurement Science and Technology.
