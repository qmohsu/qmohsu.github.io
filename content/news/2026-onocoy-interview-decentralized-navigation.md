In this interview, Prof. Li-Ta Hsu discusses **GNSS, RTK, and the future of decentralized navigation**, recorded during a visit with **onocoy** — a decentralized GNSS reference-station network co-founded by **[Lucy Icking](/people/lucy-icking/)**, a visiting PhD student in IPNL (from Hannover University, Germany). The conversation spans high-precision positioning, real-time kinematic (RTK) correction, and how decentralized reference networks could reshape access to precise navigation.
