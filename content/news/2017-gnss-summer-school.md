In July 2017, Prof. Li-Ta Hsu travelled to Tokyo as an **invited lecturer** at the **GNSS Summer School 2017** hosted by the **Institute of PNT, Japan**. Prof. Hsu delivered a session on *Fundamentals of GNSS* on 31 July 2017, contributing to the programme's curriculum on satellite-based positioning and navigation.

The GNSS Summer School is an annual training event organized by the Institute of PNT, Japan, that brings together students and early-career researchers in the navigation field. Prof. Hsu's role at the 2017 edition was as a guest instructor — IPNL was a participating speaker, not the organizing host.
